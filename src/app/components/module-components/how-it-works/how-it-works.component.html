Pet Medication Savings

Because Man's Best Friend Deserves Benefits Too!

Unfortunately, pets have many of the same health issues as humans. Those common conditions include Diabetes, Arthritis, High Blood Pressure, Allergies, Thyroid Disorders, Gastrointestinal Conditions, Mental Health, Genetic Diseases and more. And because pets have the same health issues as humans, the medications prescribed by a veterinarian is often the same meds prescribed for people. The only difference is the price at the veterinarian practice could be as much as five times more than at a retail pharmacy.

Need Help Affording Your Pet’s Medication?

If your pet is prescribed a medication that has a human equivalent, we can help! The MediPocket pet program can provide discounts up to 87% off generic and up to 20% off brand pet drugs. While certain pet medications are animal-specific, many are the same as those taken by humans. And, anytime your pet is prescribed one of these drugs, you can have it filled at a local retail pharmacy and receive the same MediPocket discounts a human would.

Just follow these steps to use the program:

  1. Get your pet's prescription from the Vet.

  2. Obtain Your Free Card: Choose 'For Pets', and either text, email, print, save it to your phone or request a physical card to be mailed to you.

  3. Use our Online Pricing Tool to find the pharmacy with the lowest price for your pet medications.

  4. Present your MediPocket Pet card in physical or digital form at a participating pharmacy along with your prescription and enjoy the savings!

For additional information about this program, visit our Help & FAQ page.
